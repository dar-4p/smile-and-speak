Classes

I teach classes on-line, at locations and at cafes. Private classes are primarily held, but in some cases, some special small group lessons may be offered.

Private lessons, in my professional opinion, provide the most effective setting for significant improvement in both pronunciation and conversational fluency. Therefore, the majority of my sessions are conducted individually. Understanding that each student possesses a unique learning style, I prioritize the cultivation of a supportive and comfortable learning environment.

Each lesson commences with a relaxed conversation centered on the student's weekend, upcoming plans, or other areas of interest, creating a comfortable learning environment. Subsequently, I engage in focused phonics practice and a collaborative exploration of lesson activities. To ensure continued engagement and reinforce learning, short, varied games incorporating pronunciation, phonics, and conversational elements are strategically incorporated throughout the lesson.

Students are welcome to ask questions regarding the lesson material or any other inquiries that support their development.

Students range from about 5 years up to adult. Any student age or level is perfectly fine.

My classes utilize a combination of original teaching materials and engaging English activities developed by myself. For more advanced learners,I also incorporate textbooks that comprehensively cover writing, grammar, reading, and listening skills. Weekly homework assignments are provided and reviewed at the start of each lesson.
