Classes

I teach classes on-line, at locations and at cafes. Private classes are primarily held, but in some cases, some special small group lessons may be offered.

I believe that private lessons offer the optimal environment for enhancing pronunciation and conversational skills. Consequently, the majority of lessons are conducted on a one-to-one basis. Recognizing the unique learning styles of each student, a strong emphasis is placed on creating a comfortable learning atmosphere.

Student Testimonial Video

Lessons are typically structured to commence with a relaxed conversation about the student's weekend, upcoming plans or other topics of interest, followed by focused phonics practice. A collaborative exploration of lesson exercises is then undertaken. To maintain engagement, a variety of short games incorporating pronunciation, phonics, and conversational elements are interspersed throughout the lesson.

The students are encouraged to ask any questions about the lesson material or any other questions to support their growth.

Students range from about 5 years up to adult. Any student age or level is perfectly fine.
